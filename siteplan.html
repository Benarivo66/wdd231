Site Plan for Online Restaurant

Site Name: Madam Rose Restaurant Services

My colleagues and I considered helping Madam Rose to build an app for her restaurant to help her reach a wider range of customers. She was our vendor and we thought of ways of helping to keep the business.

Site Purpose

The site aims to help customers learn about recipes and build confidence in the chef by learning about her qualifications and experience. It also gives a platform to interact and make orders

Scenarios

What recipes are available in the restaurant?

What is the level of qualification and experience of the Chef?

How can I make an order?

Color Schema

Color 1: #022a09. To be used as background colors for the header, headings and footers. Also to be used for borders.

Color 2: #ffffff. To be used as colors for headers and footers and also as default color for main page.

Color 3: #ECF3F1. To be used as background color for cards on the main page.

Typography

font-family: "Lora", serif. This will be used in all headings in the page.

font-family: "Outfit", sans-serif. This will be used in all regular texts that are not headings.
